Chladni Patterns

Ernst Chladni discovered that vibrating plates produce stunning geometric patterns. Sand on the plate migrates to nodal lines — regions of zero displacement — forming intricate figures.

The pattern depends on the mode numbers (n, m) which determine the vibration frequency. Higher modes produce more complex patterns.

Equation: cos(nπx)cos(mπy) ± cos(mπx)cos(nπy) = 0
